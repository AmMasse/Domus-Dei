Who Are We?

"Let me remind you, brothers, how hard we used to work, slaving night and day so as not to be a burden on any one of you while we were proclaiming God's Good News to you." (1 Thess. 2, 9 NIV)

Domus Dei is a lay apostolate organization, made up of men and women who have a zeal for evangelization...

We aim to be self-sustaining as we evangelize, helping the Church without burdening recipients, and forming faithful communities.

Our Purpose

To bring theological, philosophical and doctrinal knowledge to the faithful...

Our Goal

Better formed and informed Christians through knowledge and collaboration.

How?

  • Seminars & Lectures
  • Books
  • Media, Music, Art

Conclusion

We seek continuous faith learning and partnership in spreading the Gospel (Mk 16, 15).

Amanyire Daniel
Coordinator, Domus Dei Uganda
